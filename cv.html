Research interests

My main research focus is in theology and the arts, literature, Romanticism, and particularly the works of Samuel Taylor Coleridge (1772-1834) and Sara Coleridge (1802-1852). I have also written and presented on eco-theology, creation care, Christian Platonism, Christian humanism, film studies, and popular culture. My other related academic skill sets include course scheduling, program assessment and accreditation, course design, and advanced methods in research and writing.
